Partners

MER Lab

Marine & Environmental Research Lab is a private company specializing in the marine environmental monitoring that will lead the project, perform field validations and demonstrations, and will be responsible to set up the current meter and integrate the model to project the source and origin of spillages in real-time. 

CYENS / DeepCamera

CYENS, a center of excellence in interactive media, smart systems, and emerging technologies, will utilize artificial intelligence and machine learning to tackle thematic priority challenges. DeepCamera, comprised of professionals from various fields, will contribute fresh perspectives and experiences for AI-powered smart solutions.

Frederick Research Centre / RAS Lab

The Research team of Maritime Transport and Commerce of Frederick Research Centre, in collaboration with the Robotics and Automated Systems (RAS Lab), which has expertise in machine learning and marine robotics, will develop the Unmanned Surface Vehicle (USV). They will also lead dissemination and outreach activities and plan the commercial uptake of IRSAI innovations.
